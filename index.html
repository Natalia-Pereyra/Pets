a little bit about them

They're sexy

How do cats manage to always look so sexy is beyond me. Cats move most of the time with something that might seem like inner balance, calm, and confidence. Their eyes and their look are just so sensual and profound, anyone else agree?

They're wild

My cats love to sleep on the couch on rainy days and be all day outside when the weather is nice. Their agility, flexibility and resilience is worth admiring. Like all cats they're wonderful hunters, always bringing presents for me..usually moths and insects!

They're independent

Taking them for daily walks? Showering them? These are just some of the things you don't have to do with cats. They're extremely independent and they're clean all the time!
